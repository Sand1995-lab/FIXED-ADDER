📊 Fixed Adder Calculation Methodology

Purpose: Calculate the premium National Grid charges above commodity gas prices (NYMEX) to cover transportation, storage, administrative costs, and regulatory requirements.

Fixed Adder = GAF (Gas Adjustment Factor) - NYMEX Price

Calculation Period: August 2024 through July 2025 (12 months)
Method: Simple average of monthly fixed adders
Data Source: National Grid filed GAF rates and NYMEX commodity prices

📝 Important Implementation Notes

  • Universal Application: The $0.3350/therm fixed adder applies to ALL standard rate classes regardless of territory
  • GAF Uniformity: Gas Adjustment Factor is the same across all territories - only delivery components vary
  • Seasonal Variation: Fixed adder ranges from $0.0981 (October) to $0.6121 (November)
  • Cost Components: Includes transportation, storage, administrative costs, bad debt, and working capital
  • Regulatory Oversight: All rates subject to Massachusetts DPU approval and review
  • Exception: G-44B and G-54B use separate GAF schedule due to demand-based billing structure
💡 Usage: Add $0.3350/therm to current NYMEX prices to estimate National Grid's GAF for standard rate customers
